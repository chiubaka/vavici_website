VAVICI VENTURES

While in almost every other industry sector our financial system has driven innovations and efficiencies that have lowered costs and improved the lives of millions, this has not been true in healthcare. Exceeding GDP growth by 2.5% since 1960 the growth of health care costs is unsustainable and represents the single greatest threat to economic security the world over. Moreover with the dramatic explosion of chronic diseases and their attendant morbidities we are not healthier and things are not getting better.

Despite this harsh reality, the focus of health care investments con tinues to be on technologies and therapies that increase costs and further support rather than eliminate chronic diseases. We no longer produce treatments that even approach the “ efficient healthcare frontier ” of optimizing outcomes and costs.

Now more than ever the real opportunity and future of the market is in therapies and technologies that lower costs and improve outcomes. Patients and employers are becoming increasingly dissatisfied with the status quo. They are looking for new leadership and for solutions that make sense. Just as decades ago biotech heralded a new generation of business prosperity, health care solutions that make sense will create an even more profitable and impactful generation of companies.

Vavici Ventures, will lead the way towards this transformative change in focus. Seeking to liberate the best ideas from top academic medical centers across the country, Vavici will first take advantage of the fact that many of the solutions that make sense are already known. They've just been overlooked and so were never implemented or they were not patentable and so required a different kind of business model. In partnership with Vavici Foundation, Vavici Ventures will do the heavy lifting of creating the new companies out of models and treatments identified and developed at Vavici Foundation. Many of these will be service-based companies using best of practice algorithms that addr ess the root cause of our healthcare crisis.

Instead of focusing on increasingly complex patent protected technologies with high development costs, significant regulatory risk and long development timelines before they ever become profitable itable, Vavici will build solution oriented services, using simpler technologies, with validated algorithms that have little or no regulatory or development risk that can be profitable right away.

In so doing Vavici Ventures will generate superior returns by building health care companies that make sense, and which meet the “ efficient healthcare frontier ” (ie improve outcomes & lower costs).
