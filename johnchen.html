JOHN CHEN

Dr. Chen has over 20 years of experience in medical business development, sales and marketing, and has a proven track record of successfully building manufacturing companies in China. He is the Founder of HJY Forward Medical Investment Co. and now serving as Chairman & CEO of HJY Medical Group, focusing on building world class spine center in China. He served as CEO of Crown Dragon Medical Device Co., President of Bonovo Orthopedics Inc (US), President of Bridgetech Medicines; CEO of Mesure Technology; Co-Founder and CEO of Hunt Champion & Sunpros International; and President of Toong Yuan Co. in Taiwan and China. Prior to entering medical field, Dr. Chen also had extensive international product and business development experiences with Frito-Lay and PepsiCo Foods International in the U.S., Europe, Asia Pacific and China. Dr. Chen earned a Ph.D. from University of Minnesota.
