HAN CHIU, MD

Dr. Chiu has over twenty five years of experience in the life sciences industry as an entrepreneur, researcher, and venture investor. Most recently Dr. Chiu has focused his attention on fixing the health care system by creating new pathways for innovation and change in medicine. Vavici Ventures the first Liberty Fund is key part of this strategy. The fund concept, which was developed as part of Dr. Chiu’s capacity as Business Advisor for the Clinical Excellence Research Center at Stanford, strives to work with thought leading institutions to build and implement health care models that can lower costs and improve outcomes. VAVICI, the first Vavici Ventures company, was established to end the obesity and diabetes crisis, by enabling all primary care doctors to effectively treat this scourge.

Dr. Chiu is currently the founding and managing partner for CMX Capital, LLC, a San Diego based venture fund that is currently focused on providing advisory services to investors and life science companies. As part of the advisory service Dr. Chiu can and has taken operating positions to assist companies, including CSO and VP Business Development for Bridgetech Holdings International a life science company that licensed US biotechnologies, devices, and diagnostics for development and sales in China and Taiwan.

Prior to founding CMX Capital Fund, Dr. Chiu founded its predecessor Novel Bioventures Advisors, where he served as Managing Director and built the investment team that invested in seven companies in less than two years. His investments included Cellerant, Transmedics, Predix, ARYx, Omeros, Capnia, and Intrabiotics. In addition Dr. Chiu worked with the investment team to invest in a variety of venture and hedge funds including some of the top life science funds in the business.

He was a Venture Partner at Forward Ventures, where he sourced and evaluated numerous private equity investments and served as either Director or board observer for MedicineNet, Genicon Sciences, Arizeke, and Nereus Biosciences.

Dr. Chiu has both clinical development and marketing experience with several pharmaceuticals from two major pharmaceutical companies, Schering-Plough and Novartis. He was part of the founding oncology team for Novartis Oncology and led the effort to put together the Oncology team’s first sales force and product launch. He has launched products both domestically and internationally including Caelyx/Doxil, a product for treatment of AIDS-related Kaposi’s Sarcoma and other solid tumors; and Sandostatin, a Novartis product for the treatment of Acromegaly and GEP tumors. Dr. Chiu was the first to successfully create and implement a comprehensive and collaborative program, with a major medical society, to identify and treat patients with a chronic disease, Acromegaly.

Dr. Chiu received his M.D. from Stanford and an M.B.A. from the Haas School of Business at the University of California, Berkeley. He received his B.A. in Biochemistry from Cornell and is a member of the American College of Gastroenterology, the American Association of Clinical Endocrinologists and the Community Board of Advisors Stein Institute for Research on Aging.
