The Problem
Solution

We need to enable ALL our primary care doctors to provide the kind of root cause based treatments that can slow, halt, and ultimately reverse obesity and diabetes. Without mainstream physician engagement meaningful change cannot happen.

In contrast, whenever mainstream physicians are engaged programs move very quickly. Yet most wellness programs strive to disintermediate physicians creating more not less physician engagement.

Unlike treating symptoms a root cause based approach involves looking at many different treatment verticals. This is not a one size fits all, but rather a multi-factorial approach that require clinical judgement and creates individualized solutions.

These methodologies go well beyond traditional wellness programs enabling reversal of disease, often in less than six months.

Implementing a program to treat disease is the best way to pave the way for large-scale prevention and treatment. Once physicians see that this works, treating those with a cost effective system at earlier stages to prevent disease and improve overall health becomes natural.

VAVICI uses a state of the art web-based platform to work with thought leaders, payors, and doctors to make proper patient treatment commercially feasible. This platform empowers and engages both physicians and patients to take control and to seek and identify the specific root causes and solutions that will work for each individual patient.

The VAVICI platform is not able excluding other products and solutions, but rather empowering and enabling physicians to consider all viable products and solutions. VAVICI engages patients and physicians to work together with the data they create to challenge, evaluate and develop new and better approaches to treating patients. By working together to create collaborative patient and physician communities as well as collaborative products and services VAVICI is confident we can reverse the obesity and diabetes epidemic.
