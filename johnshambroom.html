JOHN SHAMBROOM


John has over thirty years’ experience leading professionals in the identification of clinical needs, and development of cutting edge medical devices to meet those needs in acute care and consumer wellness settings. Very early stage start-up companies are his specialty. John is currently President of Shambroom Associates, a strategic and technical consulting firm to the medical industry, with an emphasis on guiding companies through creation and validation of innovative technologies to address compelling clinical needs. Prior to this John served in multiple capacities for Zeo, Inc a consumer based sleep monitoring company that marketed the popular Zeo sleep head band. There he served as ZEO, Vice President of Scientific Affairs, Executive Director, Zeo Sleep Research Center, and Vice President, Research, Engineering, and Operations (2007 to 2009).

He wss Vice President of R&D and Operations, INOVISE MEDICAL , INC., A start-up innovating in the development of non- invasive cardiac diagnostic tools. He served as Director of Engineering and Manager of Hardware Development for ASPECT MEDICAL SYSTEMS, INC., A leading edge medical device company, that developed and marketed the world’s first Anesthetic Depth Monitor.

He was a Unit Manager for SIEMENS MEDICAL SYSTEMS and a Project Manager for LEXICON INC., a world leader in professional audio recording equipment.

John earned his BS in Engineering from from Tufts University, (cum laude) 1981 and has City of London Polytechnic, credit in Physics and Mathematics 1980.
