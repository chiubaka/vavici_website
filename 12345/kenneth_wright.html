Kenneth Wright

Ken Wright, founder of Wright Design is an accomplished innovator with over twenty years of extensive experience in product and technology development. Mr. Wright has an accomplished track record in both developing and bringing to market pioneering innovations in both the medical and consumer industries. Ken developed the original air drives ear phones, Eclipse breast monitoring devices, and provided technical guidance to the development of the movie Titanic.
