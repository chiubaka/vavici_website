James Sergi

Jim Sergi has held various positions in academic nursing and cancer research, before co-founding and serving as CEO of ProED Communications, a healthcare services and a drug and medical device development company. Jim is currently Managing Director of BayTower Medical Group, a healthcare investment advisory company, Partner in Moss, Lichen & Stone, and a member of the Board of Directors of numerous private companies. Jim also serves as an advisor to numerous healthcare companies, and has served or serves on numerous non-profit boards. Previously, Jim served as Director and Administrator of the Cleveland Clinic Foundation, Experimental Therapeutics Department within the Cancer Center, and served as the principal or co-investigator for over $40M NIH and industry funding. Jim is the author of numerous scientific papers and books related to his research and holds two patents. Jim received his undergraduate degree in nursing from the Edinboro State University of Pennsylvania and his graduate degree from University of Akron.
