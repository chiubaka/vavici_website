CANICE WU

Canice is a serial entrepreneur and investor who leverages operating experience and network to establish market leaders. He has over 27 years of experience in founding, operating, investing, and scaling successful companies with deep technology in Big Data, Software- as -a-Service business, mobile, and innovation accelerator. Passionate and experienced in leveraging innovation to tackle major challenges in numerous industries, including high technology, healthcare, financial services, telecommunications, education , and media. International experience across North America, Asia, and Europe.

Currently Canice is currently the director of corporate innovation at Rocket Space, Inc a leading global technology accelerator in San Francisco. He is also a Venture Partner at F50 a co-investment network to match fundable founders with smart capital. Featured over 100 companies raising over $50M in 1 year. He served as President and Advisor – Plug and Play Tech Center, where he transformed Plug and Play into a leading high tech business accelerator working with over 300 startups, 180 VC ’ s, and 300 corporations. He was Founder & CEO – Insights OnDemand, which provided cloud based Business Intelligence Solutions to Fortune 1000 corporations. Prior to this he was Vice President, General Manager and COO at Siebel Systems, Industry Solutions, where he grew revenue to over $300M annually. Canice was also a Management Consultant – Booz, Allen & Hamilton, where he crafted business strategies and market penetration plans for Fortune 500 companies.

Canice is also President and Board member of Asia America Mult-technology Association (AAMA), member of Sand Hill Angels, and MIT Venture Mentoring Services.

Canice has a BS and MS in Computer Science from MIT, and an MBA from University of California at Berkeley.
