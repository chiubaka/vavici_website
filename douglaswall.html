DOUGLAS F. WALL


Douglas F. Wall is the co-founder of PureForge, the first company in the world to develop brake rotors with a lifetime insurance underwritten warranty while eliminating toxic brake dust with shorter stopping distance. Called the “Oscars of Innovation”, PureForge was awarded the 2013 best mechanical systems by the prestigious R&D100 Awards. Mr. Wall is also the co-founder and lead investor for Genea Energy, a SaaS Company focused on delivering energy management solutions to commercial building owners. Doug led financings for PureForge and Genea Energy totaling over $30 million in total combined to date. Genea Energy was selected by Forbes magazine as one of America’s most promising companies. Mr. Wall is a serial entrepreneur who has successfully assisted in raising over $1 billion in capital for private companies and is the co-founder of Alitum, Mission Ventures, Shepherd Ventures, San Diego Tech Coast Angels and the San Diego Software Industry.
