DANIEL CHIU


Daniel is an empathic problem solver with strengths in logic and communication. An unwavering idealist, Daniel is impact-driven and looks for opportunities to apply himself to the world’s most important and urgent problems.

After graduating early from Stanford with an advanced Computer Science degree, Daniel joined Palantir as a Forward Deployed Engineer to work on important problems that Silicon Valley engineers are only very rarely exposed to. As a Forward Deployed Engineer, Daniel has had a unique product- and business-oriented exposure to full-stack engineering. He currently leads a small engineering team working to optimize processes for the Center for Disease Control.

Daniel’s strengths have drawn him to roles in entrepreneurship, product, and software engineering, but he isn’t wedded to a particular role or field--he tends to gravitate toward whatever and wherever growth and impact can be optimized.
