Vavici Health Solutions

Vavici Health Solutions is the first Vavici Capital Company. The mission of Vavici Health solutions is to identify and validate the best health care treatment solutions outside of the academic community whether they be from patients, physicians or other alternative health care providers and bring them into the main stream by collecting the data in a meaningful way to present to Vavici Foundation participating institutions.

Often a traditional physician will be unable to recommend or suggest a solution that a patient has found on the Internet or from an alternative health care provider, not because the solution doesnâ€™t work, but because there is insufficient published data. Even so users and patients often have useful data, but no means to express their support or to even provide this information. As a result patients often find themselves at odds with traditional doctors who in turn find themselves unable to participate in the growing interest in safer more natural solutions.

Through Vavici Health Solutions physicians and patients will again be able to work together in a structure that captures the data meaningfully and which creates a way to track and monitor safety and efficacy. Moreover other health effects and issues can be examined across large groups of patients and physicians. In many cases the numbers of patients and physicians involved may be orders of magnitude larger than traditional clinical studies.
