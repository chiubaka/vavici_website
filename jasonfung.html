JASON FUNG, MD


Dr. Fung is a Toronto based nephrologist and the founder and medical director for the Intensive Diet Management Program, a clinic that treats type 2 diabetes patients and has a successful cure rate of over 90%. He is also the author of the best selling book, Obesity Code and The Complete Guide to Fasting.

He completed medical school and internal medicine at the University of Toronto before finishing his nephrology fellowship at the University of California, Los Angeles at the Cedars-Sinai hospital. He joined Scarborough General Hospital in 2001 where he continues to practice.
