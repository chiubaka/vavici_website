Vavici Foundation

A non profit dedicated to working with a coalition of top academic centers to identify and validate health care models that will have the greatest impact on changing the course of the health care crisis.

Vavici Capital

A for profit company that will work with Vavici Foundation to convert the best health care models into viable businesses that will be funded by Vavici Capital through proof of concept.

Vavici Health Solutions

The first Vavici Capital company dedicated to identifying and validating the best health care models and solutions that are not in the academic setting and bringing them into the Vavici coalition.
