The Story

Vavici Foundation

A non-profit dedicated to working with a coalition of top academic advisors to identify and validate health care models and treatments that will have the greatest impact on healthcare by addressing root cause paradigms. more

Vavici Ventures

A for-proft venture activist company that enables a public to private initiative with the Vavici Foundation to transform the best business models and treatments into profitable businesses. Vavici Ventures will fund these proofs of concept. more

Vavici Company

The first portfolio company for Vavici Ventures, the Vavici Company, will strive to eliminate obesity and type 2 diabetes by enabling all primary-care doctors to cure their diseases by treating their root causes. more
