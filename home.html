Vavici Foundation

A non-profit dedicated to working with a coalition of top academic centers to identify and validate health care models and treatments that will have the greatest impact on healthcare by addressing root cause paradigms.

Vavici Ventures

A for profit venture activist company that enables a public to private initiative with Vavici Foundation to transform the best models and treatments into profitable businesses. Vavici Ventures will fund these to proof of concept.

Vavici Company

The first portfolio company for Vavici Ventures, the Vavici Company, will strive to eliminate obesity and type 2 diabetes, by enabling all primary care doctors to cure these diseases by treating their root causes.
