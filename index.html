What if the healthcare crisis is a lot worse than you think?

30% of medicare beneficiaries spend >50% of their retirement on healthcare and that number is growing fast

Estimated economic impact of chronic diseases in the US alone is $1 Trillion dollars

What if no one expects it to get better?

Pundits forecast a 600% increase in chronic care costs to $6 trillion by the year 2050

What if our entire focus to fixing this is wrong?

Pundits say that new treatments raise costs & often provide marginal or no real impact on quality

What if the solution to the problem were much simpler?

Healthcare experts estimate that 75% of these costs are avoidable with treatment and technologies we already have

What if there were something you could do about this?

An idea whose time has come. A family of companies dedicated to releasing life's true force by shifting the focus of health care from chronic care to wellness, preventative and curative care. Giving life hope.

Vavici Foundation

A non profit dedicated to working with a coalition of top academic centers to identify and validate health care models that will have the greatest impact on changing the course of the health care crisis.

Vavici Capital

A for profit company that will work with Vavici Foundation to convert the best health care models into viable businesses that will be funded by Vavici Capital through proof of concept.

Vavici Health Solutions

The first Vavici Capital company dedicated to identifying and validating the best health care models and solutions that are not in the academic setting and bringing them into the Vavici coalition.
