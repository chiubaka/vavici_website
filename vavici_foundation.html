Vavici Foundation

It is madness to believe that doing the same thing, investing more money into the kinds of technologies that have created and supported the growth of chronic diseases, will result in something different, wellness, prevention, and cures. Instead we need to harness the vast wealth of knowledge already amassed within the top academic centers and work together to create business models that target major chronic disease areas. 75% of all health care costs are caused by chronic diseases and it is this segment that has continued to grow dramatically and which pundits project will reach $6 trillion dollars by the year 2050 for the US alone.

Fixing this problem does not require expensive technology or new products. It requires leadership. It requires that we work together across the entire academic community. It requires that we identify, validate and build consensus around models that we can all support and implement. It requires that we be creative in building these models. It requires that we abandon ivory tower and silos thinking and work together and with the community. However, most importantly it requires that we open our eyes and hearts to all solutions we work together to make them even better.

When supported by a consensus of thought leaders medical products have historically moved into the main stream reaching 50% or greater penetration within five years or less (eg many pharmaceutical products). Without such support treatments no matter how well designed and documented rarely reach 10% penetration. Vavici Foundation is a non profit that will work with the top academic community to identify, validate and create consensus around the kinds of business models we need to shift the focus of health care back to wellness, prevention and curative care.
